About

The Pedagogy & Power digital syllabus is a project of the Educational Studies Department at Swarthmore College.  Resources include a range of digital “texts” from  art, music, literature, journalism, and other electronic sources as a means to deepen, extend, and enhance course readings and activities in the field of educational studies.
